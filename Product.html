    Directions for use

    Spray eau de parfum on skin and clothing with a generous, smooth gesture. Perfume needs heat to evaporate nicely. So all the places where it is warm, the so-called pulse points, where the blood vessels are closer to the surface, are good spots. Tips: in the neck and neckline, behind the ears, on the wrists and in your décolleté.
